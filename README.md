
# SefalYar | Designed and Developed by Amir Hossein Nazem
## Integrated Management System for Brick and Ceramic Factories

---

## 📌 What is this project?
**SefalYar** is a comprehensive management system (ERP - Enterprise Resource Planning) specifically designed and developed for brick and ceramic manufacturing factories. This system covers all management needs of a ceramic factory from production line to sales, warehousing, financial affairs, and transportation.

---

## 🤔 Why SefalYar?
In ceramic and brick factories, managing multiple departments simultaneously using manual methods or general-purpose software is extremely difficult. SefalYar solves these challenges by providing an integrated and simple solution:

| Challenge | SefalYar Solution |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Manual customer management | Complete customer information registration with purchase history and transactions |
| Lack of inventory control | Intelligent inventory management connected to goods inflow/outflow |
| Manual order price calculations | Automatic amount calculation with discount and shipping cost application |
| No connection between sales and warehouse | Automatic inventory deduction when registering orders |
| Customer financial tracking issues | Automatic updating of customer account balances |
| Lack of regular backups | Automatic and manual backup with standard formats |

## 🎯 Main Features

### 1. Management Dashboard 📊
**Central Command Center of the Factory**

A comprehensive page displaying all key business indicators:

- 💰 **Today's Sales**: For monitoring daily performance
- 📈 **Total Registered Sales**: Analyzing overall sales trends
- 🔄 **Number of Orders in Progress**: For production planning
- 💳 **Total Customer Debt**: Financial risk management
- ⚠️ **Items Below Threshold**: Reorder alerts
- 📦 **Lowest Warehouse Inventory**: Identifying critical products
- 🏦 **Top Debtors**: Prioritizing collection efforts

---

### 2. Customer Management 👥
**Intelligent Communication with Buyers**

A complete system for managing customer relationships:

**Registered Information:**
- Name and company name
- Phone number and address
- City and customer type (retailer, wholesaler, contractor, project)
- Credit limit and current account balance

**Advanced Capabilities:**
- ✅ Complete purchase history: Display all registered orders
- ✅ Financial transaction history: View receipts and payments
- ✅ Automatic total purchase calculation: Analyze each customer's value
- ✅ Advanced search: Quickly find customers
- ✅ Comprehensive profile: All information on one page

**Practical Application:**
The sales manager can quickly check each customer's status and make better decisions regarding credit approval or payment follow-ups.

---

### 3. Product Management 🧱
**Precise Inventory Control and Pricing**

Complete management of factory products:

**Information for Each Product:**
- Product name (e.g., Fire Brick 3cm)
- Product code (e.g., BR-301)
- Category (fire brick, perforated ceramic, facing brick, ceramic block, ceramic tile)
- Selling price and purchase/production cost
- Current inventory and minimum allowed stock level

**Special Features:**
- ✅ **Stock shortage alerts**: Automatic display of critical products in red
- ✅ **Connection to warehouse**: Inflow/outflow automatically changes inventory
- ✅ **Connection to orders**: Order registration automatically reduces inventory

**Practical Application:**
The production manager can know exactly which products are running out and plan for replenishment.

---

### 4. Orders & Invoicing 📦
**The Specialized Heart of the System**

The most important part of the system connecting all sections:

**Order Registration Features:**
- ✅ **Multi-item**: Ability to register multiple products in one order
- ✅ **Customer selection**: Connected to customer list (with quick registration capability)
- ✅ **Product selection**: Connected to product list (with quick registration capability)
- ✅ **Automatic calculation**: Sum of items, applying discounts and shipping costs
- ✅ **Inventory validation**: Checks sufficient stock before order registration

**Order Statuses:**
- Pending Production
- In Production
- Ready for Loading
- Shipped
- Delivered

**Payment Statuses:**
- Unpaid
- Down Payment
- Fully Settled

**Intelligent Connections:**
- 🔗 **Automatic inventory deduction**: Products are removed from warehouse
- 🔗 **Customer balance update**: Customer account is updated
- 🔗 **Warehouse recording**: A stock outflow record is automatically registered
- 🔗 **Professional invoice printing**: With exclusive design and print capability

**Practical Application:**
The sales specialist registers an order with a simple form, and the system handles everything—no manual calculations, no errors.

---

### 5. Warehouse Management 🏭
**Precise Control of Goods Flow**

Specialized warehouse management system:

**Types of Transactions:**
- **Goods Inflow**: Increase inventory (new production or purchase)
- **Goods Outflow**: Decrease inventory (sales or consumption)
- **Transfer between warehouses**: Internal movement

**Features:**
- ✅ **Connected to products**: Select product from existing list
- ✅ **Direct impact**: Each transaction automatically changes inventory
- ✅ **Date stamping**: Record exact date of each transaction
- ✅ **Supplementary notes**: Ability to record additional information
- ✅ **Reversibility**: If a transaction is deleted, inventory returns to previous state

**Practical Application:**
The warehouse keeper precisely controls inventory by recording goods inflow and outflow. The factory manager is always informed of exact warehouse status.

---

### 6. Financial System 💰
**Complete Financial Flow Management**

Management of financial transactions:

**Transaction Types:**
- **Receipt**: Collection from customers (reducing debt)
- **Payment**: Payment to suppliers or refunds (increasing debt)

**Payment Methods:**
- Cash
- Card-to-Card Transfer
- Cheque
- Bank Transfer

**Special Features:**
- ✅ **Connected to customers**: Select from list or type new name
- ✅ **Impact on account balance**: Customer account automatically updates
- ✅ **Complete history**: All financial transactions are viewable
- ✅ **Edit and delete capability**: With financial impact reversal

**Practical Application:**
The accountant is always informed of customer financial status by recording receipts and payments. Debt reports are accurate and up-to-date.

---

### 7. Fleet Management 🚚
**Logistics and Distribution Control**

Management of vehicles and drivers:

**Registered Information:**
- Driver name
- Vehicle type
- License plate number
- Load capacity (tons)

**Fleet Statuses:**
- Ready to Move
- Loading in Progress
- En Route
- In Workshop

**Practical Application:**
The transportation manager can manage the status of all vehicles and plan precisely for product distribution.

---

### 8. Backup & Recovery 💾
**Complete Information Security**

Information security system:

**Backup Methods:**
- **JSON Download**: Complete export in standard format
- **Excel Download**: Export in 6 separate sheets (professional reporting)
- **Automatic Backup**: Connect to local folder and save automatically

**Recovery Methods:**
- **Recovery from JSON**: Complete system restoration
- **Recovery from Excel**: Restoration from Excel file

**Security Features:**
- ✅ All information is stored only in the user's browser
- ✅ No data is sent to any server
- ✅ Automatic backup after every change
- ✅ Backup history for tracking

**Practical Application:**
The system administrator can back up information at any moment or, in case of issues, completely restore the system.

## 💡 Intelligent Features

### 1. Quick Registration During Work
In the orders section, the user can register a new customer or product without leaving the form. This feature increases work speed.

### 2. Intelligent Section Connections
All system sections are interconnected:

- **Order → Warehouse** (deducts inventory)
- **Order → Customer** (updates account)
- **Warehouse → Products** (changes inventory)
- **Financial → Customer** (changes account balance)

### 3. Full Reversibility
If a user deletes an order or transaction, the system automatically reverses all its effects (inventory is restored, customer account is corrected).

### 4. Automatic Backup
By connecting a local folder, the system saves a backup copy after every change. The user never worries about data loss.

### 5. Day/Night Mode
Design with support for both dark and light modes for user comfort in different lighting environments.

### 6. Professional Invoice Printing
Invoices are ready to print with exclusive design and all necessary information.
